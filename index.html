Welcome to By Design!


By Design is one of the top destinations for artistic minds in the Vancouver area. By Design sets the standard for creativity and offers incomparable expertise to help you become inspired with beautiful colours and bold ideas.

By Design offers an array of artistic tools and products curated for your ultimate inner artist. We help our customers to create masterpieces as additions to homes, as gifts and for personal mementos. Our specialty shop provides you with whatever you need from canvases, to paints, to quality brushes. We hope to unleash your inner artist soon!
